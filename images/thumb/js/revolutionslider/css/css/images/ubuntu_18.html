About XLN

What is XLN?

We are a payment method that remunerates your holders through Stakes and Masternodes
"Lunarium will seek friendly payment solutions for its entire ecosystem, with the scope of combining technological solutions with strategic partnerships to make Lunarium (XLN) a known and traded currency around the world"

Lunarium coin XLN came about after an exit scam by the former SOLARIUM coin team, we created a community-based coin aiming to continue the goals initially described by solarium coin.
